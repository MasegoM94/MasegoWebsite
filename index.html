About me

I feel like everything I have done in my life has lead to a career in data science, from my love for maths and statistics to finally doing my Masters in Data Science, I feel like the universe aligned for me to be here to do this. I believe in making people's lives easier and I think Data Science as a whole has the same aim, from the data creation to the modeling, it is meant to help people one way or another. However, this is an ever growing field and I constantly attend meetups, read articles and listen to data science podcasts to be up to date with what is relevant in the field. I decided to create this website to document my data science journey. Enjoy :)
